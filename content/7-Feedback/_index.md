# Sharing and Feedback

Participating in the **First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ)** internship program has been one of the most valuable learning experiences during my university years. Throughout the 12-week internship, I not only gained technical knowledge but also learned how to work professionally, collaborate with teammates, and solve real-world problems in a software development environment.

## Overall Evaluation

### 1. Working Environment

The working environment at FCAJ is friendly, professional, and highly supportive. Every mentor and team member is willing to answer questions and provide guidance whenever difficulties arise. Although the internship is intensive, everyone maintains a positive attitude and encourages continuous learning.

The environment helped me become more confident in asking questions, sharing ideas, and improving my technical knowledge.

---

### 2. Support from Mentor and Program Team

The mentors always provided clear guidance and valuable technical advice throughout the internship. Instead of giving direct answers, they encouraged interns to research, analyze, and solve problems independently before discussing solutions together.

The program administrators also communicated information promptly, organized workshops professionally, and ensured that every intern had access to the required learning materials and activities.

---

### 3. Relevance to My Major

The internship matched well with my academic background in **Information Technology**.

Through project development, I had opportunities to apply knowledge learned at university, including:

- Software Engineering
- Database Design
- ASP.NET Core MVC
- SQL Server
- Git & GitHub
- Software Testing
- Team Collaboration

At the same time, I gained additional knowledge about AWS cloud services and professional software development workflows.

---

### 4. Learning Opportunities

One of the most valuable aspects of this internship was the opportunity to learn beyond classroom knowledge.

During the program, I was able to:

- Study AWS fundamental services.
- Participate in technical workshops.
- Write technical blog articles.
- Develop a complete software project.
- Practice teamwork in an Agile-like environment.
- Improve documentation and presentation skills.

These experiences significantly improved both my technical and soft skills.

---

### 5. Team Culture

The team culture was open, respectful, and collaborative.

Everyone respected each other's opinions and worked together toward common goals. Team members actively shared knowledge, discussed technical issues, and supported one another throughout the internship.

As an intern, I felt comfortable communicating with mentors and teammates, which greatly improved my confidence and teamwork skills.

---

### 6. Internship Program

The internship program was well organized and provided a balanced combination of technical training and practical project experience.

The progression from AWS learning, workshops, technical blogging, and finally developing a real-world software project allowed interns to gradually improve their knowledge and practical abilities.

Overall, I believe the internship structure effectively prepared students for future careers in software development.

---

# Additional Questions

### What was the most satisfying part of your internship?

The most satisfying experience was successfully completing our project **"Pharmacy Management Website, Online Medicine Sales Platform and Customer Consultation Chatbot."**

From requirement analysis and database design to coding, testing, debugging, documentation, and final presentation, I experienced the complete software development lifecycle.

This project allowed me to transform theoretical knowledge into a working product and strengthened my confidence as a future software developer.

---

### What could be improved?

Although the internship was well organized, I believe several improvements could further enhance the learning experience:

- Organize additional hands-on technical workshops.
- Increase interaction sessions between mentors and interns.
- Provide more real-world project case studies.
- Arrange more team-building activities to strengthen collaboration.

---

### Would you recommend this internship to others?

**Yes.**

I would definitely recommend the **First Cloud AI Journey** internship program to students who are interested in Cloud Computing, Software Development, and Information Technology.

The program offers a structured learning path, practical project experience, supportive mentors, and valuable opportunities to develop both technical and professional skills.

---

# Suggestions and Future Expectations

I sincerely appreciate the effort that the FCAJ team has invested in organizing this internship program.

For future programs, I hope FCAJ can:

- Organize more advanced AWS workshops.
- Invite industry experts to share practical experiences.
- Increase opportunities for interns to participate in real business projects.
- Continue developing practical learning materials and technical challenges.

If possible, I would be very interested in joining future FCAJ programs or advanced training courses to continue improving my technical skills.

---

# Final Thoughts

Completing this internship has been a meaningful milestone in my academic journey.

I would like to express my sincere gratitude to all mentors, organizers, and teammates who supported me throughout these 12 weeks. The knowledge, experience, and professional mindset that I gained during the internship will become a solid foundation for my future career.

I look forward to applying everything I have learned to future projects and continuously improving myself as a software developer.
